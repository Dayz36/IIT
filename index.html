I will create a smartphone app that will generate recipes based off categories that you select. They will then compile the ingredients for the recipes into a shopping list. You will not be able to select specific recipes as the whole idea is to increase your knowledge of what you know how to cook so you are not stuck cooking the same 10 recipes for ever.

Motivation

Have you ever ordered one of those services that deliver to your door? The groceries have been just garbage like not even fresh. With this app you will pick the purchase the produce yourself, so you know how fresh it is. As a chef I have so many receipes stored up, the trouble my household faces is picking one of the recipes. This will remove that decision saving many of arguments!

Description

So far all I have is that you will pick meals based on categories, that have yet to be decided as to what categories will be available, if it is decided on region or comfort, but it will be multiple choice, 5 pop ups with choices of three that are randomly generated.

In this list I will have 5 choices per category, that again will be thrown into a RNG as to what recipes you end up with. It will then store quantities of the amounts into a list and display that on the screen for you to go shopping for.

Then when you go to cook it the recipe method will be stored somewhere on the app. Open it up and get to cooking!

There might be a save function where you can store your faviourite receipes that you come across in a seperate part of the app so that you can whip them out where ever you are. This is mainly so you could show others the receipes that the app has given you and maybe cook them when you are out with friends. This would be a nice feature due to free advertising and marketing when the consumer will show it to their peers.
