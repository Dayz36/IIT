Project Idea

Cooked Fresh

Overview

I will create an app that will generate recipes based off categories that you select. They will then compile the ingredients for the recipes into a shopping list. You will not be able to select specific recipes as the whole idea is to increase your knowledge of what you know how to cook so you are not stuck cooking the same 10 recipes for ever.

Motivation

Have you ever ordered one of those services that deliver to your door? The groceries have been just garbage like not even fresh. With this app you will pick the purchase the produce yourself, so you know how fresh it is. As a chef I have so many receipes stored up, the trouble my household faces is picking one of the recipes. This will remove that decision saving many of arguments!

Description

So having fresh food is seriously what makes most of the restaurant’s food so yummy. What makes this so impractical to do on a day-to-day basis is figuring out what you must do and get the shopping lists together. With this app it does all the groundwork of hunting the recipes down and compiling the ingredients into a shopping list.

It will be an app where you will pick meals based on categories, it is not recommended you do more than three as the whole idea is, we are using fresh produce. Once you have decided what categories you select the list will be generated based off a stack of recipes that I have compiled into those categories, these categories will be based on a whole bunch of different factors for example you can select food based on region or comfort or maybe your feeling fancy (Fine dining). These choices will be multiple choice so you will not get free reign of what you wish to pick, this is hopefully to make people kind of explore with their foods, otherwise you will get stuck cooking the same recipes.

Once you buy the ingredients you can then come back and select the recipe when you wish to cook it. With a method popping up in easy-to-follow text. (maybe pictures? I am a bit daunted by the code side of things)

Tools and Technologies

I am hoping just a standard mobile smartphone… I may use MIT App Inventor at this point I am not sure. I would love to write this myself, but I do not think I am at that level yet. It is doubtful that I will need a database at this point, but as this is the technology the project may require this is something that may be needed.

Skills Required

Will need strong coding experience. As well as strong research skills to find the recipes, Strong writing skills to be able to put it into text that is easily read and followed.

Outcome

If this project is successful it will just be a way to store recipes that generate on a random loop for myself at this point… I’m over cooking the same things over and over again.
